10 Reasons Why You Should Adopt A Cat

Adopting a cat is not only a wonderful way to support your local animal shelter, it's also an excellent way to bring some cuddly love into your life. Here are the top ten reasons why adopting a cat is the right move.

  1. They're independant
  2. ...yet cuddly
  3. They bath themselves
  4. You won't need to housebreak them
  5. They're avid hunters
  6. ...and great entertainers
  7. They're perfect couch potato companion
  8. You're saving a life
  9. They're saving yours
  10. They're Awesome!

You can’t argue with the facts. A cat is pretty much the most popular pet in the world (there are statistically more cats in U.S. households than dogs). They’re adorable, loving, easy companions that make you super happy (and healthy). So what are you waiting for? Head on down to your nearest shelter to find your new best friend!
